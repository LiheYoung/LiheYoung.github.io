Lihe Yang

I am a PhD student at HKU, advised by Prof. Hengshuang Zhao. I received Bachelor and Master degrees from NJU, advised by Prof. Yinghuan Shi.

Previously, I interned at Meta FAIR, ByteDance Seed, Shanghai AI Lab, SenseTime, and Tencent Youtu Lab.

I am building foundational video models toward sparse, predictive, and physically-grounded visual representations.
